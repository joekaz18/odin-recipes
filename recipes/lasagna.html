Delicious Lasagna

This is Lasagna, it is sooo awesome and yummy. It will fill you up and make you feel like a real boy!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Sauce
  • Noodles
  • Salt
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the Noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest
